IT'S ABOUT THE JOURNEY - NOT THE DESTINATION

The curiosity of merging digital arts with technology has been a constant pursuit throughout my life. I began exploring ZX Spectrum and interacting with technological devices using self-made and self-taught hardware solutions at a young age. I first encountered Kinect, Raspberry Pi, and Arduino while studying at the University of Porto in the Sound & Image program, where I created open-source project networks and interactive projects. Therefore, I have a good understanding of the transition from analog to digital and continuously develop new techniques in the computational field. I stay up-to-date with media and future developments in digitalization.





AUDIOVISUAL

I began my technical learning in Backstage Theatre Scenography, but I believed that gaining a deeper understanding of Digital Design would provide me with more perspective on light, space, form, and motion, which could be used to further develop creative ideas. Documentary and fiction sparked my early desire to create and tell stories, and I found that the generative mechanisms of digital infrastructure and editing techniques allowed me to do so effectively.

MULTIMEDIA
